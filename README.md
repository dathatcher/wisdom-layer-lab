# Wisdom Layer Lab

## Reflective Human-AI Collaboration for Complex Adaptive Systems

Artificial intelligence is rapidly increasing our ability to generate knowledge, automate work, and make decisions. Yet many of humanity's greatest challenges are not constrained by intelligence alone—they require wisdom.

The Wisdom Layer Lab is an independent research initiative exploring how AI can support human wisdom through reflection, systems thinking, complexity science, graph-based mental models, and multi-perspective reasoning.

Rather than asking:

**Can AI become wise?**

we ask:

**Can AI help humans become wiser?**
